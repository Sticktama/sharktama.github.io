HOSHIMACHI SUISEI

"It's your shooting star, your diamond in the rough, idol VTuber Hoshimachi Suisei!"

A forever 18 VTuber who deeply loves singing and idols. Her dream is to one day hold a live concert in Tokyo Budokan.

Suisei is a modest, multitalented singer and aspiring idol who is among the more level-headed members of hololive, though she also has a childish streak. She is particularly well known among fans for her skill at Tetris and her singing ability.
